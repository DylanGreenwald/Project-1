Anthony Bourdain

    Age:

    61 (June 25, 1956 - June 8, 2018)

    Short Bio:

    Anthony Bourdain was an American celebrity chef, author, travel documentarian, and television personality who starred in programs focusing on the exploration of international culture, cuisine, and the human condition. He was considered one of the most influential chefs in the world.

    Achievements:

    • In 1998, Bourdain became executive chef at Brasserie Les Halles. Based in Manhattan, NY.
    • 3 New York Times Bestsellers.
    • 4 Emmy Award Winning Shows.
